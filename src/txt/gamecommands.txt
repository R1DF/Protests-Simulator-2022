General
#HELP| - Gives you this menu.
#WHERE|, #MAP| - Shows your location on the game map.
#TIDY|, #CLEAR|, #CLS| - Clears the screen for you.

Controls
#N|/#NORTH|, #E|/#EAST|, #S|/#SOUTH|, #W|/#WEST|, #NE|/#NORTHEAST|, #NW|/#NORTHWEST, #SE|/#SOUTHEAST|, #SW|/#SOUTHWEST| - Commands for movement.
#LOOK| - Look around and see what the place offers.
#EQUIP| - Equip an item which is nearby. You can only hold two things.
#UNEQUIP| - Put back an item that you're holding.
#INV|/#INVENTORY| - Prints out what you're holding at the moment.
#STATUS| - Gives you a summary of your health.
#ME|, #DETAILS| - Gives out your full character statistics and details.
#SCORE| - Gives out your score.

Combat
#ATK|, #ATTACK| - Fight with the weapon you are currently holding. If you don't have one, you'll end up using your fists.
#DEF|, #DEFEND| - Defend yourself with a shield. If you don't have one, you'll just wing it.
#INV|, #INVENTORY| - Take a look inside what you have.
#FLEE|, #RETREAT| - Bet on your luck and flee.
